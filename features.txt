* Shows status of all your VMs
* View details about your IPs, bandwidth, memory, and HDD usage
* Tap the "Ping" area to re-ping the server and display the average ping time
* Use QR codes to quickly scan API keys when adding VMs
* Requires you to enter the VM's hostname before reboot/boot/shutdown, to ensure you reboot the correct VM
* iPhone and iPad compatible
* API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain

Security note: because your API keys are stored in your device's keychain - they're like
passwords - you will need to reenter them if you backup and restore to a new device.
Additionally, because they are stored securely, they cannot be synced between your devices.
(The developer considered adding iCould sync between devices, but decided against it for
security.)
