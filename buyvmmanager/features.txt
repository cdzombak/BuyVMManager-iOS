* Add VMs, using QR code to scan API info on iPhone
* Shows status of all your VMs
* View detailed info about your bandwidth, memory, and HDD usage
* Tap the "Ping" area to re-ping the server and display the average ping time
* View and copy all IPs assigned to your VM
* Requires you to enter the VM's hostname before reboot/boot/shutdown, to ensure you reboot the correct VM
* iPhone and iPad compatible
* iCloud syncs your VM list between your iOS devices
