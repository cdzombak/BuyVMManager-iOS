Checkup

Check on your VMs' memory, HDD, and bandwidth usage, and get an exhaustive list of their IPv4 and IPv6 addresses.

And BuyVM Manager pings the server right from your iOS device to check how it's doing.

Reboot

Reboot a runaway VM, on the road, any time. BuyVM Manager lets you reboot, shutdown, and boot VMs from your iPhone or iPad.

molly-guard style hostname confirmation ensures you never reboot the wrong VM, no matter what else is on your mind.

Get the app

What're you waiting for? BuyVM Manager is FREE, and it's a universal iPhone/iPad app.

Get started!

Get started by grabbing API keys for your VMs from the BuyVM management panel. Select a server there, and click on the "API Settings" icon under the "System Control" section.

This app is now open source: check out the Github project!

Just FYI

Grab your API keys quickly by scanning QR codes. Grab an extension like this one, and you can highlight an API key in the BuyVM Manager, right-click, and get a QR code. Then, while you're adding a new VM to BuyVM Manager, touch the camera icon next to "API Key" or "API Hash" and scan away!

Your BuyVM API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain, just like passwords. Because of that, you'll have to reenter them if you backup your device and restore the backup to a new one. It's for your own safety - really!
